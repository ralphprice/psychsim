# Memory/Representational Layer — Learning Pathways — Claude Code handover

**Build the missing learning pathways — vicarious/observational learning and modeling/imitation — so
dispositions form through the multiple routes the research documents, not just consequence-learning. This
phase is RESEQUENCED to come BEFORE the PFC↔memory loop closes**, because the diagnostic proved the loop
can't close until a *learned* control-disposition exists for the executive to present — and this phase is
what produces it. Design authority: this handover + the master learning research (Bandura social-learning
theory; observational/vicarious learning; modeling).

## 0. Critical framing (read first)
- **Current architecture only.** Episodic memory + the plug-and-play redesign remain DEFERRED (future
  parallel version). No ensemble work, no restructuring, zero risk to the working system.
- **Memory is the substrate** (Phase 1) — dispositions live in the developed plastic weights; no symbolic
  store.
- **Why this phase now (the resequencing):** the PFC↔memory presentation loop is mechanism-live but can't
  functionally close — there's no history-dependent control-*disposition* for the executive to surface,
  because the plastic PFC→control edges (vmPFC→ITC, dmPFC→LA, vlPFC→STN, vmPFC→DRN, OFC→DRN, LA→CeA — all
  plastic and ready) aren't being *driven to learn* one. The learning that would tune them is THIS phase. So
  learning pathways come first; the presentation loop then closes as a consequence.

## 1. DIAGNOSE FIRST (surface for review before building — the hard-learned rule)
A preliminary reviewer diagnostic (verified on the remote) — confirm/extend it, surface it, THEN build:
- **Consequence/RPE learning EXISTS and is solid:** the dopamine teaching signal IS `neuromod_output("DA")`
  (the live VTA/SNc output); RPE (delta = r + gamma·V' − V) gates plasticity; `ValueLearner`/BCM
  consolidation. The consequence route is built.
- **An OBSERVATIONAL SEED exists:** the Arena routes *the other's emergent act* into the perceiver's senses —
  `_perceive(other_act)` → `_add_physical_percept` → `felt_response` ("the other's emergent act physically
  presents to a perceiver's senses"). So the perceiver DOES perceive others' acts and responds.
- **THE GAP (what's missing):** the observational route feeds the other's act into the perceiver's *momentary
  response* — but there is **no vicarious learning** (learning a disposition from the *consequence to the
  other* — seeing another hurt/rewarded and tuning one's own disposition) and **no modeling/imitation**
  (no mechanism to copy/adopt another's behaviour). The perceiver sees and reacts, but does not *learn from
  what happens to others*. This is the Bandura gap.

**Confirm the gap by reading/running the code before building.** (Prior specs' premises were wrong and the
build session caught them by reading first — do the same here.)

## 2. The build (faithful to the research, integrated into the observational seed)

Bandura's social-learning theory: humans learn not only by direct reinforcement (consequence — we have it)
but by **observing others and the consequences of their behaviour** (vicarious reinforcement), and by
**modeling/imitation**. Learning is cognitively mediated — the *expectation* of reinforcement, formed
vicariously, shapes behaviour before any direct experience. In our substrate, faithfully and within the
current architecture:

### 2.1 Vicarious learning (the primary piece)
- The perceiver already perceives the other's act (`_perceive`/`felt_response`). Extend this so the perceiver
  *also* perceives the **consequence to the other** (the other being hurt/rewarded/punished — an outcome the
  Arena can present, like it presents the act), and that vicariously-perceived consequence **drives the
  perceiver's own plasticity** — tuning the perceiver's disposition as if (attenuated) it were their own
  experience. Faithful mechanism: the vicarious consequence engages the *same* learning machinery (the DA
  teaching signal / RPE-gated plasticity) but sourced from the *observed* outcome, at reduced gain (vicarious
  < direct, per the research). **The disposition is learned, not coded** — no "copy this rule"; the
  perceiver's circuits tune from observed consequence exactly as they tune from direct consequence.
- **This is what produces the control-disposition** the PFC loop needs: seeing another's defiance punished
  tunes the perceiver's plastic control edges toward "in this context, inhibit" — a *learned* disposition the
  executive can then surface. (The two phases connect precisely here.)

### 2.2 Modeling/imitation
- A mechanism by which the perceiver's observation of another's behaviour biases the perceiver's own behaviour
  toward it (imitation) — grounded in the research, built on the observational seed. Scope this carefully
  (it's the secondary piece); the primary deliverable is vicarious learning. If modeling risks scope-creep,
  it can be a following sub-step — flag to the reviewer.

### 2.3 Honesty
- **Dispositions EMERGE from the learning, never coded.** Vicarious learning tunes the *same plastic weights*
  direct experience does; modeling biases behaviour through grounded mechanism. No coded rules, no coded
  imitation targets. What is learned differs per agent by their observed experience.
- **Grounded:** any new edge/mechanism (the vicarious-consequence percept route; the observed-outcome→
  teaching-signal path) is cited (Bandura; vicarious-reinforcement neuroscience — observing others' outcomes
  engages overlapping reward/learning circuitry) and integrated into the existing felt_response/plasticity
  machinery — NOT a parallel learning module.
