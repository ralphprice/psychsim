**A 4.5-hour gate is a real cost, so the question is which items can share a gate cycle without confounding each
other's verification. The rule: items that touch different, non-interacting parts of the tree can share a gate;
items whose effects would mask or be masked by each other cannot.**

comments, the REGEN note, and the selector tests are all either record-integrity fixes (no substrate effect at

provided the gate MEASURES that the directional perversity is actually closed (OFC no longer disinhibited under
a low-SEEKING seed), not just asserts the flag is set.** Fold that measurement into the batch and it is clean.
