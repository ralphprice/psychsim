## 1. The root cause: **`LC` is missing BOTH of its major afferents**

**Aston-Jones, Ennis, Pieribone, Nickell & Shipley, *Science* 1986 — "The brain nucleus locus coeruleus:
restricted afferent control of a broad efferent network":**
- Dense focal WGA-HRP injections into LC labelled afferents in **unexpectedly few regions**. **Major inputs
  emanate from only two nuclei: the nucleus paragigantocellularis (`PGi`) and the nucleus prepositus
  hypoglossi (`PrH`)**, both in the rostral medulla. Minor: the **dorsal cap of the PVN** and the **spinal
  intermediate gray (lamina X)**.
- **`PGi` is the major EXCITATORY (glutamatergic) drive: ~73 % of LC neurons are synaptically activated by
  low-intensity single-pulse PGi stimulation.**
- **`PGi`'s function is exactly the one this substrate needs:** it integrates **exteroceptive and
  interoceptive** input — pain processing, cardiovascular regulation, respiratory control, arousal.

**Our `LC` has neither. It has `CeA` and its own α2 autoreceptor. That is the whole set.**

## 2. And `CeA`'s real status is **shell, not core** — which is why the entailment bites

The same 1986 paper is blunt about it: *"Other areas reported to project to locus coeruleus (for example,
**amygdala**, nucleus tractus solitarius, and spinal dorsal horn) did not exhibit consistent retrograde
labeling. Anterograde tracing and electrophysiologic experiments **confirmed the absence of input** to locus
coeruleus from these areas, which instead **terminate in targets adjacent to** locus coeruleus."*

**And the resolution — the reason `CeA → LC` is nonetheless real — is the shell/core distinction:** LC's
**extranuclear dendrites** extend into the peri-LC (the rostromedial and caudal juxtaependymal zones),
forming *"an extensive postsynaptic receptive surface."* **So inputs terminating "adjacent to" LC reach it
dendritically.** Van Bockstaele's CeA-CRF projection is one of them.

> **`CeA → LC` is a MINOR, DENDRITIC (shell) input. `PGi`/`PrH` are the CORE. Our model has the shell input
> as LC's SOLE afferent, at `moderate-strong`.**

## 3. So the entailment is **principle 1**, exactly

**The manipulation looks like the mechanism because a minor dendritic input is carrying 100 % of the teaching
signal's drive — and it is carrying 100 % only because the two majors are absent.** In a complete LC,
throttling the affective network would reduce NA drive **marginally**, against a `PGi` that supplies most of
it. **The entailment is an artifact of an incomplete hub, and completing the hub dissolves it — with grounded
anatomy, not by editing a set.**
