## 1. ⚠️ The ruling's premise, corrected: paths existed — through the network CU *spares*

| route | path | hops |

**But the correction makes the ruling's case STRONGER, not weaker.** `pSTS` and `rSMG-TPJ` *are* the

unbuilt, the facial half of the psychopathy signature (reduced mimicry, shallow affect) would have been
untestable, or worse, **backwards**: the spared cognitive route would have kept a CU agent's face moving.

**The right claim is not "no path" but "no path that isn't a detour through cognition."** The ruling's
conclusion — the facial route has no limbic drive — stands on that narrower, sharper basis.
